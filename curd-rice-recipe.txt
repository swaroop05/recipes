Get curd from groceries store
Get rice from groceries store
Add 3 cups of water for 1 cup of rice and soak it for 10 mins
Then add the water and rice together in a rice cooker. And switch it on
Rice cooker will indicate when its done cooking rice.
Once Rice is ready mix Rice with Curd with required consistency

Thats it. Curd Rice is ready !!..Can you believe it ;)
